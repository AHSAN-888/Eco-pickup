How EcoPickup Works

A seamless, 3-step process connecting you to a cleaner future.

1

Request Pickup

Use our app to schedule a pickup. Sort your waste, snap a photo, and hit request.

2

Captain Accepts

A nearby verified captain accepts the job and arrives quickly with smart routing.

3

Collect & Recycle

Waste is responsibly collected, processed, and reintroduced into the circular economy.

Why EcoPickup?

Next-Gen Waste Management

Packed with features to make sustainable living effortless for households and profitable for collectors.

On-Demand Pickup

Like ordering an Uber, schedule a pickup whenever you need it. No more waiting for fixed collection days.

Live Tracking

Track your assigned captain in real-time on a map, so you know exactly when they will arrive at your location.

Earn from Waste

Get paid or earn points for valuable recyclables. Turn your everyday trash into literal treasure.

Smart Segregation

Built-in AI guides to help you sort waste accurately into wet, dry, and e-waste for zero-hassle pickups.

Route Optimization

Our backend algorithm ensures captains take the shortest paths, saving fuel and time.
